# Switch

It refers to an ideal switch that connect two buses.

```{image}  /_static/Switch.svg
:alt: Switch diagram
:width: 300px
:align: center
```

The associated equations are:
```{math}
\left\{
    \begin{aligned}
        I_{\mathrm{abcn},1} &= - I_{\mathrm{abcn},2}\\
        V_{\mathrm{abcn},1} &= V_{\mathrm{abcn},2}\\
    \end{aligned}
\right.
```
